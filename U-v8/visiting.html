Visit the Expo

﻿Whether you are renovating or buying, landscaping or decorating, furnishing or building this is the show that will put you in front of the experts. With over 100 products and services in one location, over one weekend – the expo is your one stop shop for turning your home and lifestyle dreams into reality!

Compare the products and services on offer and take advantage of some amazing show only specials.

Keep an eye out on this website, subscribe to our newsletter and like us on Facebook to receive all up-to-date expo information for your visit.﻿

2019 Expo Dates

2-14 April

Entry fee

Adults: $10 per person

Gold card holders: $5 per person

Children under 16: Free

Two for one entry on Friday

*Service fees included, credit card fees apply*

Location

TSB Stadium

Mason Drive

New Plymouth

Food and Beverage

The TSB Stadium café will be open throughout the duration of the expo along with a brand new food village to tantalise your tastebuds.

Food purchased off-site may not be consumed on-site at the expo.

Getting to the Expo

TSB Stadium is situated on Pukekura Raceway within 53 acres of manicured grounds adjacent to New Plymouth's Pukekura Park.

Vehicle access - Mason Drive, off Rogan Street, New Plymouth

Parking

Free parking is available adjacent to TSB Stadium.

Parking can also be found on surrounding streets and on Rogan Street, Gover Street and Carrington Street
