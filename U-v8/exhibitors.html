Why exhibit?

﻿The Taranaki Home and Lifestyle Expo is the only expo of its kind in Taranaki and attracts over 4,000 visitors over three days.

What better way to promote your business, increase sales and extend your customer database in the Taranaki region than to book your stand for the Expo at the TSB Stadium, New Plymouth, and be part of the dynamic 2019 event calendar in Taranaki.

We're looking forward to using our extensive venue and event expertise to deliver another fantastic Expo.

Why Exhibit?

  • Strengthen or introduce your brand awareness;
  • Initiate positive leads;
  • Enhance existing customer relationships;
  • Build new relationships;
  • Expand your company database;
  • Launch new product or ideas;
  • Present your business to a captive audience;
  • Make on-the-spot sales;
  • Run competitions;
  • Research your target market;
  • Network with other businesses;
  • Affordable stall prices with easy access and parking;
  • Experienced and helpful venue and event staff.
